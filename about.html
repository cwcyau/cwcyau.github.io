About Us


We are a research group based at the Centre for Computational Biology at the University of Birmingham. The group moved from the University of Oxford in January 2017 although a number of students and postdoctoral researchers are still based in Oxford.

Our group develops statistical and computational methods for the analysis of genomic datasets with a particular interest in cancer sequencing applications and the use of Bayesian Statistics.

We are currently have projects in somatic mutation analysis of heterogeneous cancers, data fusion or integration techniques and single cell genomics.
