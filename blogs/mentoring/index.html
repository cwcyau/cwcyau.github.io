Academic Mentorship

Birmingham

Academic tutees can contact me about all aspects of their studies that do not concern academic content. This includes:

  • Issues affecting your ability to perform at your best in your studies
  • Careers advice
  • Academic references

Enquiries related to specific issues around academic content should be addressed to the lecturer/module lead in question.

All urgent or administrative enquiries should be directed to the relevant programme administrator in the first instance.

I am available for face-to-face meetings by appointment only. Contact me via e-mail (c.yau@bham.ac.uk).

Turing

I am available to support Turing-based students in the following areas:

  • Student-supervisor relationships. I have supervised numerous students to completion but I am also young enough to still remember what being a student is like! Happy to provide an ear or work on strategies with you.
  • Career planning. There are great careers out there but maximising your opportunities requires early thought and planning. Get ahead.
  • Fellowship/job application review. Need a look over a research proposal? A CV check? Just ask.

I am also happy to give informal advice on research-related matters (e.g. light review of a draft manuscript, practice presentations) on an ad-hoc basis subject to having relevant personal expertise in your area of interest.

I am generally available 0930-1430 Tuesday-Friday via Zoom. In-person meetings require a bit more advanced notice but I am typically in London once every two weeks. Contact me: cyau@turing.ac.uk
